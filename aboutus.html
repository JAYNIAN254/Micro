Our Journey

From vision to reality - the story behind Kenya's leading phone protection provider

In 2025, our founder Jayvian recognized a critical gap in the Kenyan market - the lack of affordable, reliable phone protection. Smartphones had become essential for daily life, work, and communication, yet traditional insurance solutions were complex and expensive. With a background in both technology and financial services, Jayvian assembled a team of innovators to create a solution that would make premium protection accessible to everyone.

What began as a vision to democratize device protection has grown into Kenya's most trusted phone insurance platform. Today, JayVian protects thousands of devices across the country, empowering Kenyans to use their valuable technology with confidence and peace of mind.

January 2025

Foundation

JayVian officially launched with a mission to revolutionize phone protection in Kenya. Our founder Jayvian established the core principles of affordability, transparency, and customer-centric service.

March 2025

First 1,000 Policies

We reached our first major milestone by protecting over 1,000 devices across Nairobi. This rapid adoption validated our mission and approach to microinsurance.

June 2025

Strategic Partnerships

Formed partnerships with major telecom providers and retailers to make JayVian protection available at point of sale throughout Kenya.

We continue to innovate with new protection products, enhanced digital experiences, and expanded coverage options to serve Kenya's evolving technological needs.
