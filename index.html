Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about JayVian protection

What devices do you cover?
We cover all smartphones valued up to 20,000 KES, regardless of brand or model. This includes Android and iOS devices purchased within the last 2 years.
How soon can I file a claim after signing up?
You can file a claim after 90 days of continuous premium payments. This waiting period ensures the sustainability of our protection program. After this period, you're eligible for one claim per year.
What documents do I need to file a claim?
You'll need: (1) Police abstract report, (2) Copy of your ID, (3) Proof of device ownership (receipt or box), and (4) Your device IMEI number.
How does the 3-level referral program work?

Our referral program has three levels:

  • Level 1: Direct referrals - earn 10% of their monthly premium
  • Level 2: Referrals of your referrals - earn 5% of their monthly premium
  • Level 3: Referrals of Level 2 referrals - earn 1% of their monthly premium

There's a maximum earning cap of 1,000,000 KES through the referral program.

What are the withdrawal terms for referral earnings?

When you withdraw your referral earnings, a 20% commission is applied. This commission covers:

  • Government taxes required by Kenyan law
  • Transaction processing fees
  • Program administration costs

For example: If you withdraw 1,000 KES, you'll receive 800 KES after commission.

How many claims can I make per year?
You can make one claim per year after the initial 90-day waiting period. The maximum payout per claim is 20,000 KES.
What if I want to cancel my protection?
You can cancel anytime with no penalties. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new customers. After that, you can cancel and your protection will end at the end of your current billing cycle.
